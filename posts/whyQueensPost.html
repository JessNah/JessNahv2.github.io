Why Queen's?.

Home away from home.

The january before my highschool graduation, my parents and I flew to Canada from Qatar to do a tour of all the universities that I was interested in. Queen's was definitely already one of my higher picks, not only for it being a renowned university but also because it offered something perfect for me - a common first year. Where I got to have a taste of all the different engineering disciplines.

When we visited the Queen's campus, it was breath taking and the city Kingston was sweet and safe and perfect. So while, on the campus tour, without knowing any of my own personal preferences of any sort. My dad just said "I think this is a good place for you." Now, my dad was somewhat highly against me leaving Qatar and going off to Canada on my own to study, mostly because of the whole - I was his little girl - thing. So him, mentioning that he liked somewhere thousands of miles away for me, it meant a lot to me. He was supportive, even though he didn't want to be in this case.
