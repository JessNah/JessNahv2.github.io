First week at Embross

16 month Internship begins.

The first week was really relaxed, they made an effort to not really give me much work besides a lot of documentation to read up on, which was a little boring but at the same time, it was nice to have a chance to get to know people, get familiar with the procedures people follow and just soak in the company culture. Being my usual eager self, I made sure to make use of my not much to do time, by really trying to study everything in the documentations so that if there was anything anywhere in them that would help me even a little when I got my assignment, I would have read it, understood it, and was ready. I got my own desk! I was thrilled to see it, by a window too - yey. My mentor went out of his way to be very friendly and to be very honest, everyone at the company did. Everyone was very sociable and kind and I felt right at home instantly. Remembering names sort of feels like the biggest issue at the moment haha. My manager, Ethan, was really kind and welcoming, he introduced me to everyone around which was really nice of him to take the trouble to do so, I really appreciated it. Definitely looking forward to my next 16 months.
