Me?

I'm a soon to be computer engineer, living in the oh so small town of Kingston, Ontario, Canada.

I specialize in software, with a lot of experience working as a full stack developer.

Fluent in C++, C#, C, Java, HTML5, CSS3, javascript and improving on my PHP, SQL, jQuery, and python every day.

I have a knack for adapting to new APIs and SDKs which I acquired through taking on various side projects.

Currently in my spare time, I've been learning more about machine learning, unity, and Google's new DayDream VR.

When I'm not programming, you can usually find me at the gym or painting digital artworks with my tablet. (I love art.)
